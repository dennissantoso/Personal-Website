Rate your heartbeat!

Rate your heartbeat is a mobile application that monitors your heart rate every time you open the application. It will save and keep track of your heartbeats every single day. This application will also find your minimum, maximum, and average heartbeat each day. The app is safe to use for everyone of any age.
